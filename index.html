WHY?

      

        There are three main reasons why I made this page:
        
  • BECAUSE I CAN.
    • 
        
  • IT WAS FUN!
    • 
        
  • I COULD LEARN SOMETHINK NEW.

    • 
        Do you know what quaternions are? Maybe you do, but I did not (if you feel same, dont worry, just check
        links at the end). Neither did I know how four dimensional matrix can help me create this webpage!
        This is best in trying new things: you can expect the unexpected! So if you ever wished to learn, try, or
        just read about, just do it! Do not let your curiosity wait longer.
      
    
      
    
        Below is short list of things that I learned about while makeing this webpage:
        three.js, basics of computer graphics, WebGL, shaders;
        typescript, javascript, css, scss;
        vite; npm;
        blender;
        rotation matrices, quaternions.
        You wonder what is the point of learning all of this? I doubt it is a good question. Main idea is to
        learn. If you try - you simply accomplish this objective. Attempting to solve a problem is great training even
        if you fail. However reward for making things work as you planned - that is just addictive!
